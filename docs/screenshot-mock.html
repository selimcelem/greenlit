About the job

TechFlow BV is a fast-growing Amsterdam-based SaaS company building financial-data pipelines for European fintechs. We run a small, senior platform team and are looking for a Junior Cloud Engineer to grow with us.

You'll work alongside experienced engineers on our AWS-based infrastructure, helping to maintain and evolve the platform that processes millions of transactions each day. We invest heavily in mentorship — this role is designed for someone early in their cloud career who wants to go deep.

What you'll do

  • Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines with GitHub Actions and Terraform
  • Help operate our AWS environments (ECS, Lambda, RDS, S3)
  • Improve observability — dashboards, alerts, runbooks
  • Participate in on-call rotation (shared, business hours only for the first 6 months)
  • Pair with senior engineers on incident response and post-mortems

What we're looking for

  • 0–2 years of hands-on experience with a major cloud provider (AWS preferred)
  • Comfort with Linux fundamentals and at least one scripting language
  • Familiarity with containers (Docker) and infrastructure-as-code concepts
  • Working proficiency in English; Dutch is a plus but not required
